Hands-on Training

Call for hands-on trainings closes on March 31st 2022.

All hands-on trainings will be 90 minutes in length and will be held on June 20th 2022. Depending on the quantity of trainings we select and the expected popularity of a training, you may be asked to lead the training twice.

For more information on how hands-on trainings are selected, please review our FAQ
